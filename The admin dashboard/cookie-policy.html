Cookie Policy

How BullPort uses essential, preference, analytics, support and marketing cookies in the Client Portal.

Cookie summary: BullPort uses essential cookies for security and session continuity, and may use optional cookies for preferences, analytics, support tools or marketing where lawful consent has been collected.

Last updated: July 17, 2026

1. What cookies are

Cookies and similar technologies are small files or identifiers stored on your device or browser. They help websites remember settings, protect sessions, measure usage and support product features.

2. How BullPort uses cookies

We use cookies to keep the Client Portal secure, remember preferences, understand usage patterns and, where you consent, support analytics or marketing. Strictly necessary cookies are used for basic functions such as session continuity, security and consent storage.

3. Cookie categories

Strictly necessary

Required for login, security, session continuity, fraud prevention, consent storage and basic site operation.

Preference

Used to remember choices such as region, language, display mode or interface preferences.

Analytics

Used to understand page usage, performance, errors and product improvement. These are optional where consent is required.

Marketing

Used for campaign attribution or advertising measurement if enabled. These are optional where consent is required.

Support and functional tools

Used to operate chat, support or feedback widgets if those tools are enabled.

4. Example cookie register

  • app_cookie_consent: stores cookie choices for up to 6 months.
  • app_session: supports session continuity during login.
  • app_csrf: helps protect form submissions.
  • app_locale: remembers language or region preference.
  • app_theme: remembers display preference.
  • analytics identifiers: measure usage where analytics is enabled and consent is collected.
  • support-session identifiers: keep support chat context if enabled.

5. Managing cookies

You can manage cookie preferences through any cookie controls provided in the portal and through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Updates

This Cookie Policy may be updated when BullPort changes the technologies used in the Client Portal.
