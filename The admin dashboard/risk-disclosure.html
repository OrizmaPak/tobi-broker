Risk Disclosure

Important risk information for investing, trading, portfolios, wallet workflows, demo data and options-related features in the BullPort Client Portal.

Risk warning: Investing and trading involve risk. Returns are not guaranteed. Options and other complex products can be especially high risk and may require separate approval before any live access is permitted.

Last updated: July 17, 2026

1. General investment risk

The value of investments can rise or fall. You may lose some or all of your capital. Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future performance, and projected, estimated, target or illustrative returns are not guaranteed.

2. Demo and data risk

The current Client Portal may display demo, delayed, estimated or manually maintained data. Prices, charts, portfolio values, order statuses, profit records and statements may not represent live market conditions or real account values.

3. Market and liquidity risk

Market conditions may change quickly. Some instruments may become volatile, difficult to sell, suspended, illiquid or affected by trading halts, spreads, settlement delays or market disruption.

4. Concentration and portfolio risk

Holding a concentrated portfolio can increase losses if a sector, instrument, geography or asset class performs poorly. Diversification may reduce some risks but does not eliminate the risk of loss.

5. Currency and commodity risk

Foreign currency movements, commodity prices, interest rates, inflation, geopolitical events and macroeconomic changes can affect portfolio value and income.

6. Options and complex products

Options are complex and high-risk instruments. They may expire worthless, move sharply, involve leverage, require margin or collateral, and may not be suitable for all investors.

Any options-related access shown in the current portal may be part of a demo workflow. Live access, if introduced, may require separate approval, disclosures, knowledge and experience assessment, and ongoing restrictions.

7. Wallet and funding risk

Wallet, deposit and withdrawal screens in the demo are non-operational unless marked live by BullPort. Demo balances are not proof of bank money, client money, safeguarded funds, electronic money or custodial entitlement.

8. No advice

Portal content is not personalised advice unless BullPort expressly agrees otherwise in a separate written agreement. You should consider your objectives, financial position, knowledge, experience and risk tolerance before making investment decisions.

9. Acknowledgements

By using the Client Portal, you acknowledge that investment outcomes are uncertain, that demo workflows may not represent live financial activity, and that live regulated services may require additional checks, approvals and agreements.
